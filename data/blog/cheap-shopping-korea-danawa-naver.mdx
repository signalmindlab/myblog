## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### 1. Not Checking Shipping Costs

- A lower product price might have higher shipping costs
- Always calculate the total cost including delivery
- Free shipping threshold varies by seller

### 2. Ignoring Seller Ratings

- Very low prices from unrated sellers can be risky
- Check seller reviews and ratings carefully
- Look for "ÌååÏõåÏÖÄÎü¨" (power seller) or verified badges

### 3. Missing Return/Refund Policies

- Check return windows (ÍµêÌôò/Î∞òÌíà)
- Some items (opened cosmetics, custom items) can't be returned
- International shipping returns can be complicated

### 4. Not Verifying Authenticity

- For branded products, check for official seller badges
- Compare prices - if it's too good to be true, it might be fake
- Read reviews mentioning authenticity

### 5. Rushing Due to Artificial Urgency

- "Limited time" deals often repeat
- Don't fall for countdown timers without checking price history
- Real deals will be reflected across multiple sellers
